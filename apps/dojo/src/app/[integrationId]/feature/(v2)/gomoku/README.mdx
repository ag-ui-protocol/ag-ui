# 🎮 Gomoku (Five in a Row)

## What This Demo Shows

This demo showcases CopilotKit's **shared state** capabilities through an interactive Gomoku game:

1. **Shared State**: The game board state is synchronized between the frontend and the AI agent
2. **AI Opponent**: The AI plays as White, responding to your moves using GPT-4o
3. **Real-time Updates**: The board updates instantly as the AI calculates and places its stones

## How to Play

- You play as **Black** — click any empty cell on the board to place your stone
- The **AI plays as White** and responds automatically after each of your moves
- First to get **5 in a row** (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins

## Try It Out

1. Click any cell on the board to place your first stone
2. Watch the AI think and respond
3. Try to build a winning line of 5 stones before the AI does
